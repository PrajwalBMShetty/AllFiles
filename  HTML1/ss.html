About Prithviraj Sukumaran

Prithviraj Sukumaran (born 16 October 1982) is an Indian film actor, director, producer, and playback singer, known for his works primarily in the Malayalam cinema.
He has also worked in the Tamil, Hindi and Telugu language films. He has appeared in more than 100 films in a variety of roles, and has received several awards, including the National Film Awards, Kerala State Film Awards, Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and Filmfare Awards South
